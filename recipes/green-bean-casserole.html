Green Bean Casserole

This is a great Green Bean Casserole! It's simply the BEST. And it only needs 4 simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Green Beans: Canned or Fresh, either works
  • Canned Mushroom Soup: This will help make the casserole creamy and savory
  • Cheddar Cheese: Use sharp, mild, or both
  • Fried Onions: Adds a wonderful and delicious crunch
  • Optional: Crumbled bacon, saut√©ed mushrooms or onions, garlic, Parmesan cheese, black pepper

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Mix the green beans and soup in a microwave safe bowl and microwave for 5-8 mins until warm
  2. Stir in the cheese, then mix until melted and blended
  3. Transfer to baking dish, then top with fried onions and remaining cheese
  4. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F, then bake for 10 minutes until cheese is melted and oninons are brown
