Stuffing

After having this stuffing, your family will be positively stuffed

Ingredients:

  • 1 lbs of White Bread
  • 3/4 cup of butter
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 4 stalks chopped celery
  • 2 teaspoons poultry seasoning
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Let bread slices dry for 1-2 hours, then cut into cubes
  2. Melt butter in dutch oven or saucepan, then add onion and celery and cook until soft. Season with poultry seasoning, salt, and pepper
  3. Stir in bread cubes, then pour in chicken broth. Mix well
  4. Chill, then bake in a buttered casserole dish at 350 degrees F for 30 to 40 mins
