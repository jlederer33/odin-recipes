Baked Brie

This baked brie is the perfect appetizer

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet frozen puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 (8 ounce) wheel of Brie
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and lightly grease pie pan
  2. Slice brie in half horizontally into two flat wheels
  3. Put puff pastry into pie pan, then place one wheel of brie on it. Top with almonds
  4. Place second wheel of brie on top, then cover with pastry
  5. Bake in preheated over for 15 to 20 minutes. Let cool for 5 mins before serving
